About Us


We are a team of researchers with international experience and having expertise of many years in the preparation of nano-materials. We assure our buyers with superior quality of products and on time services. We deliver what we promise and believe in building a better buyer-seller relationship.

NanoRex is one of the very few leading manufactures and suppliers of high purity Nano- materials powder and dispersions in India. We deal in all kinds of customizable Metal/Metal- oxide nano-particles and Quantum dots.

Nanorex has made its name as a superior and trusted supplier of an array of nano-materials within buyers and collaborations in more than 10 countries including Belgium, Spain, Germany ,Ukraine, Singapore and Australia. We supply nano-materials in quantities as low as  1gm to as high as 10 Kg and more. Our products are assured of being in complete assent with the international standards and are prepared using the finest quality of chemicals and other basic materials.
